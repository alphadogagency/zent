Why Choose Zent Family Dentistry

Quality dental care for Mishawaka families

Conveniently located just 10 minutes from Mishawaka via Main Street or Lincoln Way, Zent Family Dentistry brings you comprehensive dental care without the long drive. Drs. Stephen and Sheena Zent are a husband-and-wife team who have been serving the greater Michiana community since 2012, with a focus on conservative, preventative dentistry using state-of-the-art technology.

What We Offer

Comprehensive care for your whole family

From routine cleanings to full-mouth restorations, Mishawaka families trust Zent Family Dentistry for exceptional dental care just minutes away.

Cleanings & Prevention

Exams, digital X-rays, fluoride treatment, oral cancer screenings, and sealants.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain veneers, teeth whitening, and smile makeovers to enhance your confidence.

Crowns & Fillings

Restorations that look natural and stand the test of time.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-feeling replacements for missing teeth.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly care that makes visits fun and stress-free.

Clear Aligners

Straighten your smile discreetly with modern clear aligner orthodontics.

Root Canals & Extractions

Expert endodontic and oral surgery care when you need it most.

Botox & TMJ Relief

Therapeutic and cosmetic Botox for pain relief and facial aesthetics.
