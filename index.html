Meet the Doctors

Stephen & Sheena Zent

Dr. Stephen A. ZentDDS Dr. Sheena K. ZentDDS

Conveniently located on Douglas Road in South Bend between SR-23 and Grape Road, Drs. Zent are dedicated to providing their patients with the highest quality care by practicing conservative, preventative dentistry. They believe in a comprehensive approach to treating patients’ oral health with the goal of providing healthy smiles that last a lifetime.

Drs. Zent are standing members of the American Dental Association, Indiana Dental Association, and North Central Dental Society. They enjoy keeping up with new technology as well as continuing education.

Procedures

Comprehensive care for
your whole family

Whether your dental needs are a complete exam and cleaning, a full-mouth restoration, or anything in between, we promise to provide you with exceptional care — with a gentle touch, and stunning results.

Cleanings & Prevention

Exams, digital X-rays, fluoride, sealants, and VelScope® oral cancer screenings.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain veneers, cosmetic bonding, custom take-home whitening trays, and more.

Crowns & Fillings

Tooth-colored composite fillings, porcelain and zirconia crowns, bridges, and dentures.

Dental Implants

Titanium posts that act as a sturdy, long-lasting anchor for replacement teeth.

Pediatric Dentistry

Starting early is the key to a lifetime of good dental health. First visit within 6 months of the first tooth.

Clear Aligners

Invisalign, SureSmile, and Spark — virtually invisible aligners with free consults.

Root Canals & Extractions

Simple, surgical, and root tip extractions performed with care — or referred when needed.

Botox & TMJ Relief

Botox, Jeuveau, Xeomin, and Dysport for esthetics, TMJ pain, clenching, and migraines.
